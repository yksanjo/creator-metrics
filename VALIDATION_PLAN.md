## Phase 2: Customer Interviews (Days 3-14)

### Recruitment Strategy

**Target Profile:**
- Micro-influencers: 10K-100K followers
- Mid-tier creators: 100K-500K followers
- 50% video creators (YouTube/TikTok)
- 50% image/text creators (Instagram/X)
- Mix of niches: lifestyle, tech, fitness, gaming

**Recruitment Channels:**
1. Landing page signups (offer 15-min call for free month)
2. Twitter DMs to creators
3. Reddit outreach
4. Your personal network

**Outreach Template:**
```
Subject: Quick question about your analytics workflow

Hi [Name],

I'm building a tool to help creators track their analytics across platforms in one place.

I saw your content on [Platform] and would love to learn about your current analytics workflow.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call? Happy to offer a free month of the tool when it launches as a thank you.

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Interview Script (15 minutes)

**Introduction (2 min):**
```
"Thanks for taking the time. I'm building CreatorMetrics - a unified analytics dashboard for content creators.

This isn't a sales call - I'm trying to understand if this is actually a problem worth solving. I'd love to hear about your current workflow and what's frustrating about it.

Do you mind if I record this for note-taking purposes?"
```

**Current State (4 min):**
1. What platforms are you active on?
2. Walk me through how you check your analytics currently.
3. How often do you look at your numbers?
4. What metrics matter most to you?

**Pain Points (4 min):**
5. What's the most frustrating part of tracking your growth?
6. Have you tried any tools to solve this? What happened?
7. If you had a magic wand, what would your analytics workflow look like?

**Solution Validation (3 min):**
8. I'm thinking of building [describe solution]. Would that solve your problem?
9. What features would be must-have vs nice-to-have?
10. If this existed today, would you pay $19/month for it?

**Closing (2 min):**
```
"Thanks so much for your time. This is incredibly helpful.

Can I keep you in the loop as I build this? And would you be interested in being a beta user when it's ready?"
```

### Key Questions to Answer

| Question | Why It Matters |
|----------|----------------|
| Do they check analytics regularly? | Validates problem severity |
| How many platforms do they use? | Validates multi-platform need |
| What tools have they tried? | Competitive intelligence |
| Would they pay $19/month? | Price sensitivity |
| What feature is most important? | MVP prioritization |

### Documentation

Create a spreadsheet tracking:
- Creator name/handle
- Follower count range
- Platforms used
- Current tools
- Top pain point
- Price sensitivity
- Interest level (1-10)
- Key quotes

## Phase 3: Beta Program (Weeks 3-6)

### Beta User Selection

**Criteria:**
- Engaged during interview
- Expressed strong interest
- Mix of platform types
- Different follower ranges

**Goal:** 20 beta users

### Beta Program Structure

**Week 1: Onboarding**
- Welcome email with setup instructions
- Connect their platforms
- 15-min onboarding call (optional)

**Week 2-3: Active Usage**
- Check in mid-week via email
- Collect feedback via Typeform
- Monitor usage analytics

**Week 4: Feedback Collection**
- In-depth feedback survey
- NPS score
- Feature prioritization vote
- Testimonial request
